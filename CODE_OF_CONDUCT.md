# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the VibTools open-source community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, education, or personal characteristics.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and technically constructive.
- Critique code and decisions without attacking people.
- Respect repository scope, maintainer decisions, and security boundaries.
- Protect private information and report sensitive security issues responsibly.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, deliberate disruption, publication of private information, and malicious security disclosures are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `hello@vib.tools`. Maintainers may edit, hide, reject, or remove contributions and may restrict participation when necessary to protect the community and projects.
